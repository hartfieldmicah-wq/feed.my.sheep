Our Mission

"He said to him the third time, 'Simon, son of John, do you love me?' Peter said to him, 'Lord, you know everything; you know that I love you.' Jesus said to him, 'Feed my sheep.'"
— John 21:17

This is how we reach His Sheep.

We exist to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ with every person we encounter, driven by the belief that no one is too lost, too broken, or too far gone for the love of God. We go not out of obligation, but out of overflowing joy for what He has done in our own lives, seeking to encourage the discouraged, walk with the hurting, and lead others toward the peace found only in Christ. With humility and boldness, we strive to make every word and action a reflection of His goodness so that hearts may be softened, lives renewed, and His kingdom grown one soul at a time.

The Need — Why We Go

The Harvest Is Plentiful, But The Laborers Are Few

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you." — Matthew 28:19–20

Our world is filled with people searching for hope, identity, and a home. Whether they are students on a campus, individuals navigating life on the street, or people in your own neighborhood who feel broken and overlooked, they are all hungry for the truth of the Gospel. We are not waiting for them to walk into a building. We are taking the love of Christ directly to the places where they live, struggle, and breathe. This is not a project. It is a promise to never look away from the people Jesus came to save. May the Lamb receive the reward of his suffering.

What We Do — The Operational Reality

Intentional Ministry, Measured Impact

"I am the vine; you are the branches. If you remain in me and I in you, you will bear much fruit; apart from me you can do nothing." — John 15:5

Without God, we are nothing. What we set out to do is for Him and through Him.

We bridge the gap between initial outreach and long-term discipleship through a model designed for every person we encounter. We do not use a one-size-fits-all approach. We meet people where they are through targeted engagement:

  • Tactical Outreach: We distribute curated Outreach Kits. Whether we are on a college campus, a city street, or a park, we carry the right resources to meet the moment. Our kits are designed to serve everyone from those in physical need to those seeking purpose or navigating personal crises. Every kit contains both physical and informational resources alongside the good news of Jesus.
  • Relational Discipleship: We do not just hand out items and leave. We are trained to sit, listen, and build genuine rapport. We create a safe space for people to share their stories and hear the life-changing truth of Christ.
  • Bridge to the Body: We view ourselves as a gateway ministry. Our goal is to connect those we meet with local, Bible-believing churches that can provide the community, mentorship, and support needed for lasting life change.
Our Ask — Support & Fundraising

Partner With Us In The Harvest

"How, then, can they call on the one they have not believed in? And how can they believe in the one of whom they have not heard? And how can they hear without someone preaching to them? And how can anyone preach unless they are sent? As it is written: 'How beautiful are the feet of those who bring good news!'" — Romans 10:14–15

Will you send us?

We are looking for partner churches and gospel partners to help us sustain this mission. We do not want to be an isolated island — we want to be a resource that helps the entire body of Christ become more active in the mission field.

  • Equip the Mission: We are raising support to stock our Outreach Kits. A gift of $25 allows us to assemble a kit tailored to the specific needs of the person we meet, ensuring we always have a Bible and the right resources ready to go.
  • Mobilize the Church: We want to partner with your congregation. We provide the logistics, the safety protocols, and the training. You provide the hearts and the hands. We are ready to host evangelism training workshops for your youth and adult groups to get them equipped for the front lines of every mission field.

Roadmap

Where God is taking us, step by step.

Step 01

Meet with Anchor Point Church to share our vision, discuss our plans, and seek the support and covering we need to move forward in faith.

Step 02

Reach out to pastors and churches we already have relationships with, casting the vision and building a network of like-minded believers ready to move.

Step 03

Document our next evangelism day — capturing stories, moments, and the fruit of stepping out in faith so others can see what God is doing.

Step 04

Produce a short documentary from our evangelism day to share with partner churches — inspiring others to go and do likewise.

Step 05 · The Goal

Spread God's Love — and watch Him do exceedingly abundantly above all we could ask or imagine.
