Our Mission

We are on a heartfelt mission to spread the love of Jesus Christ to every person we are blessed to encounter. We believe wholeheartedly in sharing the Gospel — the good news of the salvation, grace, and new life that Jesus freely offers to every one of His children. No one is too lost, too broken, or too far gone for the love of God, and we carry that truth with us wherever we go.

Through service, prayer, genuine relationships, and bold outreach, we seek to be a steady light in our community and beyond. We desire to encourage the discouraged, walk alongside the hurting, and lovingly lead people toward the peace and freedom that can only be found in Christ. We go not out of obligation, but out of overflowing joy and gratitude for what He has done in our own lives.

With humility, compassion, and faith, we want our every word and action to reflect His goodness — so that hearts may be softened, lives renewed, and His kingdom grown one precious soul at a time.

"He said to him the third time, 'Simon, son of John, do you love me?' Peter said to him, 'Lord, you know everything; you know that I love you.' Jesus said to him, 'Feed my sheep.'" — John 21:17

Roadmap

Where God is taking us, step by step.

Step 01

Meet with Anchor Point Church to share our vision, discuss our plans, and seek the support and covering we need to move forward in faith.

Step 02

Reach out to pastors and churches we already have relationships with, casting the vision and building a network of like-minded believers ready to move.

Step 03

Document our next evangelism day — capturing stories, moments, and the fruit of stepping out in faith so others can see what God is doing.

Step 04

Produce a short documentary from our evangelism day to share with partner churches — inspiring others to go and do likewise.

Step 05 · The Goal

Spread God's Love — and watch Him do exceedingly abundantly above all we could ask or imagine.
